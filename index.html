My Favorite Music Moments of High School!

  1. Memory #1: Jazz Orchestra Concerts!

    I loved perorming in every one of my jazz concerts the past two years. The highlight of last year was playing with the trombone section where we were all good friends outside of music. This year was a different vibe- we all still got along just in a different way. The biggest difference this year is that they sound amazing and it's a lot of a fun getting to play in a band of that calliber.

  2. Memory #2: Concerto Concert!

    The Concerto Concert in March was super fun. The orchestra felt like a real community. I also felt like we knew the music better than any other concert, so the music ended up being super expressive and powerful (especially the Sibelius Violin Concerto!) Plus, in that cycle we had a collaboration with the NY Phil and I had a blast playing with and learning from that veteran section.

  3. Memory #3:Juilliard Senior Recital!

    My senior recital was tough as it took so much work, but it totally paid off and was super satisfying and rewarding. My family finally got to hear me play since it was my first ever recital. I had a lot of fun working with my teacher and my accompanist as well as the brass sextet. Being in that chamber group may have been my favorite musical experience so far.
